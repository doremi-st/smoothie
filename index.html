🌋

Ingredients

🎯 Booster Juice Berry Bloom match — banana adds creaminess, no honey needed. Naturally mild sweetness.
Wednesday
🌿
Mankey's Branch Blend
Mankey · Fighting Type
Ingredients
🥑 Frozen Avocado ¼ cup 🥭 Frozen Mango ½ cup 🌿 Spinach ½ cup 🍊 Orange Juice ½ cup 🥛 Plain Yogurt ¼ cup
🌱 Add spinach first, blend before adding fruit. Pour into an opaque cup — the green disappears behind the mango flavour.
Thursday
👊

Lucario · Fighting-Steel Type
Ingredients

⚠️ Ground almond meal only — never whole almonds (choking hazard). Tastes like a mild blueberry milkshake.

🥑

🩷 Avocado makes it silky-creamy. Spinach hides completely behind the strawberry colour. No honey needed — naturally balanced.
Saturday
👾
Guzzlord's Beast Blend
Guzzlord · Dark-Dragon Type
Ingredients
🥭 Frozen Mango ½ cup 🫐 Frozen Blueberry ¼ cup 🥜 Ground Pecan 1 tbsp 🍊 Orange Juice ⅓ cup 🥛 Plain Yogurt ¼ cup
⚠️ Ground pecan meal only — pulse in a dry blender first. Toasty pecan depth cuts the sweetness of the mango perfectly.
Sunday
🪽
Archen's Defense Blend
Archen · Rock-Flying Type
Ingredients
🍓 Frozen Strawberry ½ cup 🫐 Frozen Blueberry ¼ cup 🥭 Frozen Mango ¼ cup 🍌 Banana ½ 🥛 Milk ½ cup 🍯 Honey ½ tsp (opt.)
⭐ The crowd-pleaser — Theo's guaranteed win on picky days. Taste before adding honey; it's usually sweet enough without.
🎒 Trainer Tip: Pre-pack 7 freezer bags on Sunday (all ingredients except milk/yogurt/OJ). Label each with the Pokémon name. Honey is optional in all recipes.  |  ⚠️ Nuts: Always ground meal only — never whole pecans or almonds for Theo.
